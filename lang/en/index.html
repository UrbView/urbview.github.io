THE PROBLEM

The lack of safety in public spaces that affects some groups more than others is a major obstacle on the road to gender equity.

OUR VISION

Creating an environment in which people of all groups feel safe enough to move around freely without restricting themselves in their freedom of experience of the city.

OUR WORK: AI and Street Harassment Prevention

Empowering cities and citizens with AI

Combining Urban Planning with Artificial Intelligence and Crime Prevention

We use data of places where people feel and don't feel comfortable to identify possible patterns in the design of the spots that can be associated to street harassment.
This serves as a tool to make city designs more inclusive for everyone.
