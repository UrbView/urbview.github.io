The idea behind UrbView goes back to very emotional, personal experiences. We, as the founders of UrbView, care deeply about the physical and psychological wellbeing of our friends, our families and any citizen all around. We know what it feels like to be afraid to walk the streets in urban environments in which we do not feel safe. And we know how much damage street harassment can do. Throughout our studies, we have focused specifically on issues like sexual violence, street harassment and crime in public spaces. The urgency of these problems that we perceived not only in society at large but as part of our personal history as well, is what brought us together and led to the creation of the UrbView project.

OUR WORK: Combining Urban Planning with Artificial Intelligence and Crime Prevention

Empowering cities and citizens with AI

Combining Urban Planning with Artificial Intelligence and Crime Prevention

With the help of UrbView, city planners, designers and architects can evaluate the safety of their design and are provided with the tools and suggestions to change it accordingly.
Its use will significantly improve urban environments and help reduce the occurrence of street harassment and crime rates overall.
